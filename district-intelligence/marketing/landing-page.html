How It Works

Four intelligence streams, one score

1

Board Meeting Analysis

We fetch transcripts from public board meeting recordings and use AI to detect budget discussions, complaints, and technology mentions relevant to your vertical.

2

Bond Measure Tracking

Every passed bond measure is parsed for AV, technology, and construction scope. Bond passage is the single strongest buying signal in K-12.

3

LCAP Filing Analysis

California's Local Control Accountability Plans reveal exactly what each district plans to spend on technology — a year before the purchase order.

4

Leadership Change Alerts

New superintendents, CTOs, and facilities directors reset vendor relationships. We surface every change within 72 hours of news publication.

All four streams combine into a single 0–100 Opportunity Score per district. Your team only needs to look at the top of the list.
